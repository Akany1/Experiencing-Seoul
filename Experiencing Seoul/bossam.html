Taste Traditional Bossam

Tender steamed pork wrapped in fresh vegetables — a comforting dish that captures the essence of Korean cuisine.

Bossam is a beloved Korean dish made from gently boiled pork belly, sliced and served with crisp vegetables, fermented kimchi, and savory sauces. Each wrap delivers a perfect balance of flavors and textures — soft, spicy, fresh, and rich — making it a must-try for anyone exploring Seoul's food culture.
